## What are your favorite languages?
I really enjoyed the flexibility of working in C. With languages nowadays, they are so hand holdy and abstracted that they don't let you make mistakes. For some, this is okay, but with that level of abstraction comes limitations to a language that ends up creating too complex of tech stacks (looking at you Javascript/React and Java).

For object oriented languages, I quite enjoy Python. It has its own quirks, but does what it is meant to do extremely well.
